About PTE

Computer-Based Test

The PTE exam is a fully computer-based test that assesses your English language proficiency.

Global Recognition

PTE is accepted by thousands of universities, colleges, and governments worldwide.

Flexible Scheduling

You can take the PTE exam year-round at test centers located in over 50 countries.

PTE Test Format

Speaking

Speaking test assesses your spoken English through tasks like reading aloud, describing images, and discussing topics.

Reading

Evaluates your reading comprehension and ability toanalyze written material.

Listening

Assesses your listening skills and ability to understand spoken English.

Writing

Writing test assesses your writing skills through two tasks: a task 1 academic or general training writing task and a task 2 essay.

Tips for PTE Success

Practice Regularly

Familiarize yourself with the test format and question types through consistent practice.

Enhance Vocabulary

Build a strong vocabulary to accurately express your thoughts and ideas.

Manage Time Wisely

Develop time management strategies to complete all tasks within the allocated time.

Stay Calm

Approach the exam with a positive mindset and avoid unnecessary stress

PTE Scoring Bands and Test dates

Scoring bands

Academic test scores range from 10 to 90, with 90 being the highest.You receive an overall score and individual scores for each skill: Speaking, Writing, Reading, and Listening

Test Dates

PTE Academic tests are conducted on various dates throughout the year at authorized Pearson Test Centers.

Registration

Register online or through a local test center.
