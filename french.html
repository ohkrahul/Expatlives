French Certifications

DELF

DELF - Diplôme d'études en langue française

The most widely recognized French certification worldwide!

  • ✨ Levels: A1 to B2
  • 📜 Internationally recognized
  • ⏳ Valid for life
  • 🎯 Perfect for academic & professional goals

What you'll be tested on:

Listening Reading Writing Speaking
DALF

DALF - Diplôme approfondi de langue française

Advanced certification for mastery of French!

  • 🌟 Levels: C1 and C2
  • 🎓 University admission
  • 💼 Professional recognition
  • 🌍 Global acceptance

Advanced competencies:

Academic Writing Debate Research Presentation
TCF

TCF - Test de connaissance du français

Quick evaluation of your French proficiency!

  • 🔄 Valid for 2 years
  • ⚡ Quick results
  • 📱 Digital testing
  • ✈️ Perfect for immigration

Test format:

80 Questions 1h30 Duration Adaptive Testing
