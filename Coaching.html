IELTS vs PTE: Choose Your Path

IELTS

Duration: 2 hours 45 minutes

Format: Paper/Computer-based

Speaking: Face-to-face interview

  • Widely accepted globally
  • Human examiner for speaking
  • Band score 0-9
  • Multiple test dates
  • Academic & General Training
OR

PTE

Duration: 2 hours

Format: Computer-based only

Speaking: AI-based assessment

  • Faster results (24-48 hours)
  • AI-powered scoring
  • Score range 10-90
  • Flexible scheduling
  • Integrated skills testing
