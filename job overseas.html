🔍 Our Step-by-Step Placement Process

We simplify the journey from candidate to global professional through a streamlined and transparent recruitment process:

1

Career Consultation & Registration

Submit your resume or register online. Our consultants will assess your skills, qualifications, and aspirations to recommend suitable job openings.

2

Job Matching & Shortlisting

We work with a network of verified international employers. Based on your profile, we shortlist roles that align with your career goals and experience.

3

Interview Scheduling & Coaching

Our team arranges and prepares you for interviews—helping with mock sessions, interview etiquette, and country-specific expectations.

4

Documentation & Visa Assistance

We assist with all necessary paperwork, including:

  • Work permit applications
  • Visa processing
  • Medical tests
  • Background verification
5

Pre-Departure Orientation

We equip you with everything you need to adapt smoothly to your new environment—cultural awareness, legal rights, employer expectations, and local living tips.
